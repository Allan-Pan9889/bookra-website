Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2025

Introduction

Bookra ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using Bookra, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
  • Account credentials: Encrypted password for secure access

2. User Content

We collect and store content you create within the App:

  • Book data: Book titles, authors, ISBN codes, covers, and metadata
  • Reading notes: Highlights, annotations, and personal notes
  • Reading progress: Current page, completion percentage, reading status
  • AI-generated content: X-Ray analyses, recommendations, and insights
  • Quote cards: Custom-designed quote images you create

3. Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your use of the App:

  • Feature usage: Which features you use and how often
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version
  • App performance data: Crash reports, error logs

4. Camera Access

The App requires camera access to scan book spines and ISBN barcodes. Photos are processed locally on your device for text recognition and are not uploaded to our servers unless you choose to save book covers to your library.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the App: Enable core functionality including book library management, scanning, and AI features
  • Generate AI insights: Process your book data to create X-Ray analyses, recommendations, and connections
  • Personalize your experience: Tailor recommendations based on your reading history and preferences
  • Improve our services: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Customer support: Respond to your questions and provide assistance
  • Security: Protect against unauthorized access and fraudulent activity
  • Legal compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

Data Storage and Security

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-leading cloud service providers and is protected by enterprise-grade security measures. Our service providers comply with international data security standards.

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure email/password authentication with encrypted credentials
  • Access control: Row-level security policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Regular backups: Automated backups protect against data loss

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the App, including but not limited to:

  • Cloud service providers: For data storage and authentication services
  • AI service providers: To generate book analyses and recommendations
  • Payment processors: For in-app purchase processing (they handle payment information directly)

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders or government agencies).

AI Processing and Third-Party Services

Our AI-powered features (X-Ray Analysis, Recommendations, Themed Paths) use third-party AI services to process your book data. When you use these features:

  • Book metadata (title, author) may be sent to AI providers for analysis
  • Processing happens in real-time and data is not retained by AI providers
  • Generated insights are stored in your account and encrypted
  • All AI-generated content is clearly labeled in the App

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View all personal data we have about you
  • Update your data: Edit your profile information and book library
  • Export your data: Download your library and notes in CSV format
  • Delete your data: Permanently delete your account and all associated data

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise these rights:

  • Access Settings > Data & Privacy in the App
  • Contact us at support@bookra.im

Account Deletion

When you delete your account, all personal data, book libraries, notes, and AI-generated content are permanently deleted within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@bookra.im.

International Data Transfers

To provide our services, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in different countries or regions. We take appropriate security measures to protect your data and ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws. By using the App, you consent to such data transfers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Sending you a notification through the App
  • Sending you an email (for material changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
